Welcome!

El Cafetero Bakery is a Colombian restaurant and bakery in White Plains, New York. We feature an authentic Colombian bakery, in addition to serving delicious native Colombian food. Whether you're in the mood for breakfast, lunch, a beverage, or a light snack, we have the food and the prices to satisfy you. Come try Colombian coffee, or stop by and savor something new. We do parties and catering as well!
